Who are we?

Sushi specialist company.

We respect the product and the environment.

Responsible Consumption

Responsible consumption is important to us, therefore our products never exceed the number of orders in order to avoid food waste.

100% Recyclable

All our packaging is made from 100% recycled cardboard. In addition, the paper we use to protect the product inside the box during shipment can be reused to preserve them once they arrive in your kitchen.

24h delivery

To guarantee that the quality of our product remains intact in your home, we have a 24h delivery service. Your order will be dispatched within a maximum of 24 hours.
